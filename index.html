Game Jam FAQ
When does the game jam start?
The jam kicks off on August 16, 2025 (Monday).
How long does the jam run?
It’s a 2-week term break activity—perfect for anyone looking for something fun and productive during the break.
Who can join?
Anyone who wants to create, experiment, and have fun! Teams can have a maximum of 2 members.
Is there a prize?
Yes! The winning team gets ₱500—enough for a nice lunch. But honestly, the prize is just a bonus. The real goal is to enjoy the process and learn something new.
Do I have to focus on winning?
Not at all! This jam is all about having fun, trying your best, and creating something cool. Winning is just the cherry on top.
What should I expect?
A relaxed, creative environment where you can make games, connect with others, and challenge yourself during your break.
How do I submit my game?
Details on submissions will be provided closer to the jam start date. Just keep an eye out!
